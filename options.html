Settings

Timeout

Recommended: 500-3000

Timeout is the tolerance to slow typing. It is the maximum number of milliseconds tolerated between keystrokes in a single macro.

In other words, if you type :smi, wait a bit, and then type le:, the :smile: macro will be recognized only if the pause was a duration of time less than or equal to timeout.

Lookbehind

Recommended: 1-4. Must be >0.

Lookbehind is the tolerance to fast typing. It is the number of characters that will be checked behind the cursor for macros in order to account for occasional dropped keys.

The extension will sometimes miss keystrokes. If you type :cowboy:, the final : may be missed, and so the macro will not be recognized. To account for this, the extension will look lookbehind characters behind the caret for macros.
